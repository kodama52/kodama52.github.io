Hey there, I'm a UX/UI Designer with a unique blend of creativity and entrepreneurship . I love tackling design challenges and creating solutions that make a positive impact on people's lives.

My design process starts with a keen eye for detail and a deep understanding of user needs. I'm always observing and listening to people's problems to gain insight and inspiration. I then sketch my ideas on paper before creating digital models using software like Figma and SolidWorks.

I'm not just a designer - I'm also a strategic team leader. As the founder of a competitive esports team, I led my team to many victories by creating innovative strategies on the fly and working collaboratively with my teammates.

In my free time, you'll find me experimenting with new technologies and gadgets or participating in competitive activities that challenge me both mentally and physically. I'm always seeking out new opportunities to learn and grow, and I believe that this mindset is essential to creating truly great designs.

If you're looking for a designer who is passionate, creative, and always up for a challenge, I'd love to connect with you. Drop me a message and let's chat!
