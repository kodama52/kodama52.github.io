About Jonathan

I am a UX/UI Designer with a creative entrepreneurship mindset. Having a hybrid outlook I am able to understand every client's needs and design with that in mind. I'm a self-starter and proactive in anything I find myself doing. My designing inspiration comes from my natural curiosity to learn more & observing people with their struggles throughout the day. I love to imagine new and creative ways to improve current processes.

I am a strategic team leader. During my undergraduate program, I founded a competitive esports team. As a team captain, I led my team to many vicotries by creating spontaneous & fluid strategies during each match by coordinating with each of my teammates. Outside of work you can find me tinkering & researching emerging technologies as well as taking part in competitive activities.

If you'd like to talk about a project or just chat feel free to email me.
